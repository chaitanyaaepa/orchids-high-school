Frequently Asked Questions

Common Admission Queries

Find quick answers regarding our admission standards, fee formats, curriculum, and safety measures.

What is the teacher-to-student ratio at OHS?

Our teacher-to-student ratio is kept at 1:12 in Pre-Primary modules and 1:15 across Primary, Middle, and High School classrooms. This ensures personalized attention and active developmental monitoring for every child.

Which board curriculum does the school follow?

Orchids High School aligns with a hybrid curriculum standard: following national academic standards integrated with the Cambridge International System for science, digital coding courses, and language frameworks.

Are transport systems safe and tracking-enabled?

Yes. All OHS buses are GPS-enabled, equipped with real-time digital cameras and safety guards. Parents can monitor live travel routes and receive automated text notifications when their child boards or departs the bus.

What sports activities are offered to pupils?

We provide training coaches for soccer, basketball, swimming, lawn tennis, table tennis, badminton, gymnastics, chess, and competitive track athletics. Sports are integrated as mandatory units inside weekly timetables.

How does the admission process work?

The system is direct: 1) Fill the online registration form. 2) Visit the campus for child assessment and parent interaction. 3) Submit documentation transcripts and secure admission approval.
